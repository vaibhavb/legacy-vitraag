Objective
    The goal of  Sensor Networks @ CHLA is to build a wireless sensor network platform for sensor fusion and real-time multisensor processing and adaptation. The initial application will be MUSIQ (MUltimodality in-situ alcohol SensIng and Quantitation system).


Grant
    “Multimodality in-situ alcohol sensing and quantitation system
(MUSIQ)”, funded by NIH/NIAAA. June 2003 - June 2008.
