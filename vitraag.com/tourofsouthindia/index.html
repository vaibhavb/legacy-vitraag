Tour Of South India.

our goal:

While discovering our home-land through 2100km of bicycling in 21 days, we want to journal our experiences and make an attempt to furthur the cause of education!

About the tour!

Tour de South India is a cycling tour from Chennai to Goa. Participants -- Vaibhav, Tullika and Rahul. Cause -- Asha for Education and Teach for India.

Our Cause

Our good friend Milind quit his corporate job to teach for india. TFI and Milind has been an inspiration to the new generation and we want to do our little part in supporting their cause.
